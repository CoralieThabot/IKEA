The Omniwomen UK + Allies Leadership Summit is taking place on International Women's Day – next Friday 8th March.

Omniwomen UK + Allies has been on a mission for the last five years to help our organisation increase the seniority and influence of women in its leadership roles. This year's theme is Celebrating and Supporting Diverse Paths to Leadership, and those lucky enough to be at the Summit will be hearing from some amazing speakers about this topic.

However, it's not just the attendees who can experience the talks this year – we're going to be live streaming the first three keynote speeches at Bankside 3, 7th Floor, Warehouse Space, 09:00 – 10:20, as well as on our social channels.

We would love your help to spread the word about the mission that Omniwomen UK + Allies are on, and to let your agency know about the free live streaming event, which they can sign up to on Eventbrite here: https://omniwomenuk.eventbrite.co.uk.
